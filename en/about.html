About me

Hi! I‘m Nieves and I consider myself as a “language-holic” and a culture lover.

Regarding my education, I studied the Degree of Modern Languages, Communication and Culture, specialied in English and Chinese. Then, I studied a distance-learning master in International Economic Relations at The Global Business School. Nowadays, I study a master in Intitutional translation at University of Alicante.

My passion for languages has provided me great communicative skills (intrapersonal and interpersonal). Today, I am bilingual in English and Spanish, I have a high level of French (C1), upper-intermediate level of German, Chinese and Portuguese (B2).

Regarding my career, I work as a Computational Linguist in Fonetic (Madrid). In addition, I have worked as a language assistant and communication liaison by doing some translations ina brokerage, at Rey Juan Carlos University and at Beaumont School (high school in St. Albans,UK).I have also worked for the Confucious Insititute.

I have translated some webpages such as Hostal Valdepeñas website, and during my Erasmus I translated the book Ucebnice cínské konverzace (Chinese-Spanish). Finally I have collaborated as voluntaty translator for United Nations Volunteers Perú.
